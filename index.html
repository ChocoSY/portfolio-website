SLAN

Saving huge amount of money sounds stressful and complicated
but with personal financial assistant,
SLAN, in your hand, the process of saving will become simple,
and it will give confidence to users. We consider every user's need seriously
so the strategy of saving will be planned differently by each case to provide more personalized experience.

Cookease

Cookease is a recipe web app which allows users who are interested in cooking
to share and to write their recipe ideas.
This will let users give or get help with cooking,
from preparation of ingredient to making delicious meal.
Users can trust this web app since they are asked to add their profile
which can keep users' words and attitudes appropriate to others.

view project

Better d.

Better d. is the app that users can use any where and any time to track their productivity.
Users can add tasks and record how long it takes them to finish each task and the total time.
They can also create a group where each member can view other members' task lists, and this will keep users motivated.

view project

MoCord

MoCord is a diary app which can be used in various ways
such as journaling, scheduling, or memo etc.
It is developed to let users have similar experience of using physical diary.
So this app will satisfy users who want to try digital diary
but want to keep the feeling of using physical diary.

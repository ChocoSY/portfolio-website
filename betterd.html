Productivity APP

Better d. is a productivity app.
It targets age of 16-24 in which uses productivity app for studying in most cases. Therefore, it is designed to give the best experience of focus without evoking stress.

Project Develop Summary

IDEATION

I selected 3 apps that I was using for a market research, and gathered the points that will be useful for this project. The final selectiong of essential functions were; Sign-in & Sign-up, Create Task, Record Progress, Share Tasks with Others, Mark Completion.

WIREFRAMES

With 5 main functions, user flow was created, and followingly, the low-fidelity.

DESIGN

Productivity app is mostly used to achieve a goal and achieving goal is a part of making dreams come true. And the word 'Dream' gave me the image of 'Cloud' and it was selected as the main image. Different shades of blue were chosen for the color palette to mix well the streotypical color of cloud with calmness which is one of main moods. The deisgn of buttons and typing spaces was kept as simple as possible to decrease any factors that can trigger disturbance.

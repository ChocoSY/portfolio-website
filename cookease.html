Recipe App

Cookease is a responsive web app.
It introduces various types of recipes.
I performed both UI and UX portions in this project.

Project Develop Summary

IDEATION

Based on market research and pre-development stage user interview, I created user personas. Three personas were set up, ages ranging from the 20s to 50s from different places around the world. Since a recipe app should be accessible to anyone, I gave each persona a different set of technological skills.

WIREFRAMES

After user interviews and a market search, I came up with eight functions. And I divided them into five musts and three needs. With five musts, I created low-fidelity wireframes and developed them into mid-fidelity wireframes.

DESIGN

The keywords for the mood of this app were simple, warm, neutral, and focus. The colors that matched these moods were cream, ivory, yellow, and orange. To continue expressing this mood on buttons and typing spaces, I chose a round corner square for them. For typography, I picked Arima Madurai, which is sarif, to give a hint of classiness.

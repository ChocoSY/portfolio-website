Budget APP

SLAN is a handy budget app.
I had a concrete idea about this project from the beginning. So, I started off with 5 low-fidelity wireframes and in the end, created 12 high-fidelity wireframes.

Project Develop Summary

IDEATION

I created a user flow diagram and a low-fidelity wireframe with four functions requested in the project brief. A clearly drawn user flow diagram helped me focus throughout the development process. Based on this user flow diagram, I could create a low-fidelity diagram. I first planned to create a mobile app. So these wireframes have some features of a mobile app such as iOS' bottom navigation bar.

WIREFRAMES

From the low-fidelity wireframes, I developed them to mid-fidelity wireframes. I could start solidifying my design ideas on the screens.

DESIGN

To imply seriousness and simplicity, I set black and white as the main colors. As the accent color, I chose sand, red and green. For typography, I chose sans-serif with medium kerning.
About the logo, the dotted half rim represents the progress of saving and the solid half rim represents the end of saving. And 'S' at the center means the dollar sign, $, and the first letter of the word 'saving'.

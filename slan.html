SLAN

Budget APP

SLAN is a handy budget app.
I did not follow traditional path of low-mid-high-fidelity wireframes for this project, instead,
I started from 5 low-fidelity wireframes and ended up as 12 high-fidelity screens which low fidelity wireframes of other 7 screens do not exist.

My Process

IDEATION

Including the minimum functions requested in the project brief, I created user flow and low-fidelity wireframe.
The minimal functions and elements will give users ease of use.
Also, it only asks for essential amount of personal information which will make users feel the web app as a reliable one.

WIREFRAMES

From the low-fidelity wireframes, I developed them to mid-fidelity wireframes which I can see the direction of the overall screens I want to design.

DESIGN

To imply seriousness and simpleness, I set black and white as the main colors with some other accent colors
such as sand color representing gold and red and green representing money going out and in.
For typography, I chose sans-serif, medium kerning font.
The logo of this web app represents the progress and saving where dotted half rim represents the progress and the solid half rim represents completeness.
'S' at the center has two meaning, one, dollar sign, $ and second, 'saving'.

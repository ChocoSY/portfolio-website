Diary APP

MoCord is a diary app.
The purpose of this app is to reduce the gap between a paper diary and a digital diary. It supports both iOS and Android.

Project Develop Summary

IDEATION

Nowadays, planners are transforming into digital forms. But there are still quite a large amount of people who insist on using a paper form. I tried to follow this trend of transition, converting from paper to digital screen. But I figured that it is not that simple. I found the pain points in existing planning apps. And I could start working on this app by gathering some featuring functions.

WIREFRAMES

Following each operating system's typical outline, I created low-fidelity wireframes. For example, for iOS, menus are on the bottom navigation bar. And for Android, the menus are hidden in the hamburger menu button which is on the top navigation bar.

DESIGN

I chose each system's default color palette and fonts as I was creating native apps. Also, I designed other UI elements following each system's design rules.

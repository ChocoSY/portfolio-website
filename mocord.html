Diary APP

MoCord is a diary app.
It is devised to reduce the gap between paper-diary and digital diary. It supports both iOS and Android.

Project Develop Summary

IDEATION

Even though a lot of note takings are turning into digital form, there are still quite large amount of people who insist on using paper form. I found the pain points on existing diary apps on the market and set some essential functions and one special function.

WIREFRAMES

With clear user flow, few low-fidelity wireframes were created, following each system's typical outline.
For example, for iOS, menus are on the bottom navigation bar, whereas, for Android, the same menus are hidden in the hamburger menu button which is placed on the top navigation bar.

DESIGN

The colors and fonts were set based on each system's basic color palette. Also, most of other design elements follow each system's design rules.

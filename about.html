About Me

Where I am based

I was born in Korea and I am based in BC Canada currently.

My professional experience

I majored in Mathematics, BSc at the University of British Columbia. I chose to study mathematics as I loved (and I still love) dealing with numbers and wanted to become a teacher. While majoring in Mathematics, I minored in the Japanese language as well.

Fun fact about me

Since I am a UI designer, it is easy to mistake me for a computer or game lover, something that deals with technology. However, I am fond of handwriting, old school. I've had a deep interest in stationeries since I entered elementary school. And that interest made me carry a notebook and a pen any time and anywhere. I like recording every single moment of my life in written form. I organize my thoughts and feelings by jotting them down, doodling, and decorating pages with craft items.

How I spend my free time

I like reading books, especially in Japanese. It helps me practice language skills. I also like driving even though I am a newbie driver. When I am stressed, I grab my car key and wallet and go on a drive. It helps me put all my thoughts and emotions out of my mind for that moment. And also, nobody cares whether I sing loud or burst into laughter in the car.
